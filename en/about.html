About Me

I am a hybrid professional specializing in Graphic Design and Video Editing, with over 8 years of experience transforming concepts into high-impact visual content for digital platforms.

Expert in creating promos, ads, and marketing assets, my approach combines technical precision with narrative intent. I integrate motion graphics and visual effects organically, always prioritizing rhythm and emotional connection with the audience.

With a strong command of Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects, my current edge lies in the strategic integration of AI tools into production workflows, allowing me to streamline processes and elevate the final quality of every project at scale.

I am passionate about visual storytelling that not only captures attention from start to finish but also aligns with the brand's strategic goals.
