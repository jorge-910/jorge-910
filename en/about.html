About Me

I am a graphic designer specialized in video editing and motion graphics, with more than 8 years of experience creating visual content for digital platforms.

I have worked on promos, ads, and marketing assets, with close attention to pacing, intent, and final quality. I integrate motion graphics and visual effects as a natural part of the story, not as decoration.

I have strong command of Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects, plus hands-on experience integrating AI tools into production workflows to improve efficiency and raise output quality.

I am driven by visual storytelling that makes an impact, connects with the audience, and keeps attention from start to finish.

Strengths

Time management and the ability to handle multiple projects at once, while maintaining quality and meeting tight deadlines.

Solid foundation in design principles and digital marketing strategies, applied to results-oriented content such as A/B testing and dynamic creative.

Experience leading, supporting, and mentoring junior designers, helping both technical growth and creative judgment.

Strong ability to integrate AI effectively into design and video editing workflows, optimizing production and scaling output without compromising quality.
