PSC

Multimedia strategy and execution for the Partit dels Socialistes de Catalunya (PSC) through agency Lemon & hijos, leading the visual narrative of its digital channels during the complex health crisis period (2020-2022).

Production of high-impact social media content, integrating advanced motion graphics and static visual systems designed to ensure clear, rapid, and consistent institutional communication.

Management of political and strategic communication, transforming complex directives and government messages into visually effective informational pieces, adapted to a context of crisis management and maximum public exposure.

Technical direction in the development of graphic system and motion graphics for the 14th PSC Congress, creating a dynamic visual identity applied across large-format digital media and audiovisual event productions.

This project demonstrates my ability to work under critical timelines, maintaining institutional rigor and technical excellence in the communication of major organizations.
