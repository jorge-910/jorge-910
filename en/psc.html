PSC

Project developed at agency Lemon&hijos for the Partit dels Socialistes de Catalunya (PSC), managing the party's Instagram channel during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Production of graphic and audiovisual content for social media, combining motion graphics and static creatives based on communication needs.

Posts covered institutional communication, political messaging as the opposition party, and especially public information and recommendations aligned with central government (PSOE) guidelines during the health crisis.

I also developed visual graphics and motion graphics for the 14th PSC Congress, applied across digital formats and audiovisual assets.
